Passport Act 2020

Key Highlights

Section 301: Every citizen of the Republic of the Marshall Islands shall be entitled to a passport, subject to the conditions of this Act.

Section 302: A passport shall be issued only upon application in the manner and form prescribed and shall include personal details as required.

Section 303: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade is the designated authority for the issuance of passports under this Act.

Section 309: The passport remains the property of the Republic and must be surrendered upon demand by the issuing authority.

Section 310: Any person who makes a false statement or provides fraudulent information in a passport application commits an offense and is liable under law.

Section 311: The Minister may by regulation provide for the types of passports issued, fees, validity, renewal, and other provisions.
