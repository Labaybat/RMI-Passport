Passport Application Portal

Welcome to the official online passport application system for Marshallese citizens.

Before You Apply:

  • You must be a citizen of the Republic of the Marshall Islands
  • Prepare a recent passport-style photo (digital)
  • Upload a copy of your birth certificate or valid Marshallese ID
  • Make sure all information submitted is accurate and true
Start Application

Important Legal Notices (Passport Act 2020)

  • All passport applicants must be Marshallese citizens (Section 301).
  • False or misleading statements in your application are punishable by law (Section 310).
  • Passports remain the property of the Republic of the Marshall Islands (Section 311).
  • The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade is responsible for issuing passports (Section 303).
