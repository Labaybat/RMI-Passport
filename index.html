Passport Act Highlights

  • Types of Passports: Regular, Official, and Diplomatic.
  • Eligibility: Only lawful Marshallese citizens are eligible.
  • Application Review: Conducted by a designated Passport Review Panel.
  • Supervision: The Attorney General oversees all application processes.
  • Legal Consequences: Providing false information is a criminal offense.
