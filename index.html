Republic of the Marshall Islands – Passport Portal

Before You Apply

Please review the following legal responsibilities before submitting your passport application:

  • You must be a lawful Marshallese citizen to apply.
  • Providing false or misleading information is a criminal offense under the Passport Act 2020.
  • Applications are reviewed by a designated Passport Review Panel under the Attorney General’s supervision.
  • Supporting documents verifying your identity and eligibility are required.
