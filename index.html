Passport Act 2020 – Key Sections

Defines key terms like Attorney General, Director, Passport Division, and lawful citizen. Forms the legal foundation of the Act.

Establishes the Passports and Citizenship Division, appoints staff and outlines their roles, led by a Director under the supervision of the Attorney General.

Three passport types: Regular (10 yrs), Official (5 yrs), Diplomatic (5 yrs). Each has strict eligibility and return policies upon job termination or expiration.

Lists required documents, eligibility criteria, review process by the Director and Panel, and specific rules for citizens by birth, naturalization, or investment.

Criminalizes selling, forging, or using false info in passport applications. Bribery, obstruction, and failure to surrender documents are punishable by fines or imprisonment.
