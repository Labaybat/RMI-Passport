Republic of the Marshall Islands

Official Passport Application Portal

The Passport Act 2020 governs the secure issuance of Regular, Official, and Diplomatic passports to lawful citizens of the Republic.

Only verified Marshallese citizens may apply. Applications are reviewed under the supervision of the Attorney General and a dedicated Passport Review Panel.

Forgery, bribery, or submission of false information is a criminal offense under national law, punishable by fines or imprisonment.

Regular passports are valid for 10 years; Official and Diplomatic for 5 years.
