Before You Apply

Please ensure you have read and understood the following key legal responsibilities before submitting your application:

  • You must be a lawful Marshallese citizen to apply for a passport.
  • Providing false or misleading information is a criminal offense under the Passport Act 2020.
  • All applications are reviewed by the Passport Review Panel under supervision of the Attorney General.
  • You may be required to provide supporting documents verifying your identity and eligibility.
